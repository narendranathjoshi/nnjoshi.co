Sanjana Shankar, My Sanjana Shankar

Will you be my Valentine?

You. Who is smart. Who is pretty. Who is slightly weird.

You. Who is intelligent. Who is sensible. Who has always for me cheered.

You. Who is romantic. At times, who is so unromantic. Who is funny.

You. Who is cheerful. Who is encouraging. Many a time, who has made my cloudy day sunny.

You. Who makes fun of me. But I don't mind. Who annoys me. But I don't mind. Who is sometimes mean to me. But I don't mind.

You. Whom I want to play games with. Whom I want to cook for. Whom I want to talk to. Because you're one of a kind.

You. In whose hug I'd bury myself. Whom I'd wrap my arms around and snuggle. I'm so glad this is all true.

Do you wanna know why?

Because I love you.

Will you be my Valentine?
